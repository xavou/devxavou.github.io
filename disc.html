Albums

My World 2.0 (2010)

Includes the global hit “Baby” and established Justin as an international superstar.

Believe (2012)

Marked a more mature sound with hits like “Boyfriend” and “Beauty and a Beat”.

Purpose (2015)

Critically acclaimed for singles like “Sorry”, “What Do You Mean?”, and “Love Yourself”.

Changes (2020)

Focused on R&B influences, featuring “Yummy” and collaborations with top artists.

Justice (2021)

Featured the global hit “Peaches” and the Billboard #1 collaboration “Stay” with The Kid LAROI.
