Overview

Justin Drew Bieber (born March 1, 1994) is a Canadian singer and songwriter who rose to fame as a teenager after being discovered on YouTube in 2008. He quickly became a global sensation with his debut single “One Time” and breakout hit “Baby”.

Over the years, Bieber has evolved from a teen idol into one of the most influential pop and R&B artists in the world. His career highlights include multiple Grammy Awards, chart-topping albums, and collaborations with some of the biggest names in the music industry.

Known for hits such as “Sorry”, “Love Yourself”, “Peaches”, and “Stay”, Justin Bieber continues to inspire millions of fans worldwide. Beyond music, he is also active in philanthropy and often uses his platform to promote mental health awareness and global causes.
