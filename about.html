About Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and performer who rose to fame as a teenager with his debut single One Time. Since then, he has become one of the most influential global pop icons.

With chart-topping albums like My World 2.0, Purpose, and Justice, Bieber has consistently redefined pop and R&B. His music explores themes of love, fame, faith, and redemption, resonating with millions worldwide.

Beyond music, Justin has used his platform to raise awareness on mental health and social causes, showing his growth both as an artist and an individual.
